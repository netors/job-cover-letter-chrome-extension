The Real Story

I was applying to jobs and realized I was spending hours crafting cover letters for each application. Some were good, others... not so much. But I was using ChatGPT anyway to help brainstorm ideas.

Then it hit me: why not just automate this? I'm already copy-pasting job descriptions into ChatGPT, then tweaking the output. Why not make it seamless?

So I built this Chrome extension over a weekend. It automatically grabs job details from any job site, sends them to AI (your choice of OpenAI or Claude), and generates a tailored cover letter instantly.
